About

These days, we are told by individuals around us that we can learn anything online and that that is advantage we have compared to generations before us who did not have access to the internet.

However, we are almost never provided a clear roadmap of how to adequately utilize the internet to learn topic of our interest.

That's where my webpage comes in. With my webpage, anyone interested in learning a certain aspect of Software Engineering a way to easily access curated material on the topic of their choice to learn effectively.

Impact!

Where can this problem have lasting effects?

Career Development

Difficulty in learning can hinder individuals' ability to acquire new skills, potentially limiting their career advancement and employability.

Where else else can this problem have lasting effects?

Long-term Learning Habits

Struggling to find a clear roadmap might create negative learning habits, such as procrastination or reliance on superficial learning, which can affect lifelong learning.
